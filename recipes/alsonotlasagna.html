Lasagna

Description

Cheesy, hearty, and EASY. This lasagna recipe is a quick meal suitable for any size family.

This is just another line to practice the HTML paragraph stuff.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Tomato Pasta Sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Mozzerrella Cheese
  1. Bring water to a boil and start oven to 350 degrees
  2. Brown gorund beef
  3. Add pasta to boiling water for 9 min
  4. Mix cheese and sauce with burger in the pan
  5. Strain noodles and add to pan
  6. Place in oven on center wrack for 20 min
  7. Enjoy!
